Research

My research is aimed at understanding theoretical and computational problems in stochastic processes (specifically in biological systems). I focus on optimizing processes to reach desired targets in finite time, applying strategies such as Stochastic Resetting. In a biological context, I work on virus assembly and disassembly, particularly studying how capsid assembly and disassembly occur under different physiological conditions.
